The Custom Advantage.

Today, anybody can make a website with Wix, seemingly for free. But top businesses know that the best strategy is to get a custom-made website.

Why? Because custom sites look and feel better. When you've taken the time to make a website that fits your business/organization, your customers feel appreciated. Rather than having to navigate a website cluttered with the genericism of a website template, your customers' experiences can be focused and efficient.

Not only that, because templates from sites like Wix have to fit a bunch of different organizations, they have a lot of bells and whistles in their internal code. So they inherently load slowly. But custom websites can be incredibly lean, and because of that, they're more responsive and load much more quickly, leading to a better customer experience.

But the biggest concern any business has is that its potential user base has to be able to find the business. No users, no business. So the techniques of search engine optimization (SEO) are critical to ensuring that your site is listed on the top pages of Google and other search engines. Interestingly enough, SEO works best with custom websites—sites hosted like platforms like Wix often suffer because they're associated with the massive space of Wix websites, so it's harder for the search engine to differentiate them.

Make your business known, and leave a distinct impression in your customer's mind with the searchability, responsiveness, and elegant simplicity of a custom website. Go custom.

Design for the Modern Age.

We're in 2016, but too many website designers still pretend we're in 2004.

Their websites still use outdated technologies and paradigms, like Adobe Flash, tables in the use of their web design, and gradients/bevel techniques as elements of their design. Together, many of these elements are often described as the "HTML4" framework. Websites using HTML4 don't work well on mobile devices, and they're ugly, slow, and often even dangerously insecure—technologies such as Flash can seriously threaten the safety of your data and of your customers' devices.

HTML4 is an absolutely obsolete standard. Avoid the mistake of having your website made within this inferior framework, which not only sacrifices the quality of your users' experiences, but the reputation of your business itself. Instead, get your business's website created by AG Design Services to make it look immersive, modern, flat, and responsive on mobile.
